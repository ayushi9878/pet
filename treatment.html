Effective Treatment for Canine Ear Infections

Ear infections are common in dogs, especially those with floppy ears.

  • Symptoms: Scratching, redness, bad odor.
  • Causes: Bacteria, yeast overgrowth, or allergies.
  • Treatment: Vet-prescribed ear drops, regular cleaning.

How to Treat and Prevent Canine Arthritis

Arthritis causes joint pain and reduced mobility in older dogs.

  • Symptoms: Stiffness, limping, difficulty climbing stairs.
  • Causes: Aging, genetics, excessive weight.
  • Management: Joint supplements, pain relievers, weight control.
